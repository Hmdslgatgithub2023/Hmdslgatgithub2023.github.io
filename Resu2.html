: About Dr. Maryam Hashemi



Dr. Maryam Hashemi, Is a specialist in radiology, ultrasound, CT scan, and MRI, a member of American, European, and Iranian radiology associations, graduated with a doctorate in general medicine and a specialized doctorate in radiology, ultrasound, CT scan, and MRI

She Is a holder of a a fellowship degree in gynecological, pregnancy, and fetal imaging from Tehran University of Medical Sciences

She is a holder of a of a fellowship degree in breast imaging and intrusion from Tehran University of Medical Sciences

She is a holder of an IOTA international specialized certificate. furthermore, she has participated and is actively present in the annual specialized radiology congresses of Europe ECR. and She also has participation and active presence in annual radiology congresses of FMF, ISOUG

University teaching and attending as a guest speaker on behalf of Tehran Medical Sciences Universities

Cooperating with the most prominent specialized hospitals in Tehran, such as Tandis Hospital (located in Jordan), Laleh Hospital (located in Sharak Gharb), Bahman Hospital (located in Sharak Gharb)

Cooperating with the Women's Health Ultrasound and Radiology Institute (located in Gandi)
